# Izimpande Health Ledger

## Overview

Izimpande Health Ledger is a digital health record platform designed to improve healthcare accessibility, continuity of care, and wellness management.

The platform provides a secure and centralized system for managing health records, medications, appointments, and wellness information for individuals and care institutions.

The project is inspired by the need to improve healthcare record management and access to health information in underserved and vulnerable communities.

---

## Vision

To create a trusted digital health ecosystem that empowers individuals, caregivers, and healthcare professionals through secure, accessible, and transparent health record management.

---

## Problem Statement

Healthcare information is often fragmented across paper files, clinics, institutions, and caregivers. This can lead to:

* Lost medical records
* Poor continuity of care
* Difficulty tracking medications
* Limited access to healthcare information
* Administrative inefficiencies

Izimpande Health Ledger aims to address these challenges through a modern digital solution.
